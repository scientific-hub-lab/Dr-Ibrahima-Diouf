Welcome

Hello — I’m Dr. Ibrahima Diouf

I work at the intersection of climate and public health. My interests include climate-sensitive diseases, rainfall and CMIP6 projections, malaria risk, and decision-ready dashboards for West and Central Africa. I teach and collaborate with the University Cheikh Anta Diop (Senegal) and the University of Labé (Guinea).
